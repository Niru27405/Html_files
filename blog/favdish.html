Margherita Pizza

Classic homemade pizza served hot and cheesy.

📋Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt, to taste
  • Chili flakes (optional)

👨🏻‍🍳Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the pizza base.
  3. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella on top of the sauce.
  4. Add basil leaves and drizzle olive oil over the pizza
  5. Sprinkle oregano and a pinch of salt.
  6. Bake in the oven for 10–12 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly
  7. Serve hot, garnished with chili flakes if desired.

📌Tips

For a crispier crust, bake the pizza on a preheated pizza stone or tray. You can also add other toppings like olives, bell peppers, or mushrooms.
