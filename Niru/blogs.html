📂Projects

Automated Public Lighting

Designed a smart street lighting system using LDR and IR sensors. Lights glow at 40% when dark and switch to full brightness upon detecting movement, enhancing energy efficiency and safety.

Wireless EV Charging

Developed a wireless electric vehicle charging system using inductive coupling. Integrated Hall effect sensors to detect misalignment and enhance charging efficiency. A microcontroller manages the system and displays charging status via an I2C LCD for real-time monitoring.

Solar Panel Tracking System

Developed a solar panel tracking system using light sensors and a PID controller to dynamically adjust panel orientation toward sunlight, maximizing energy efficiency compared to static systems.
